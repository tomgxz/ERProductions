Overview

ER Productions:
A Pioneering Laser, SFX & Pyro Specialist

ER Productions is a multi-discipline production effects provider, specialising in the design and delivery of tailored laser shows for high-profile global events, including music tours, television production and corporate events. The company has also recently expanded its services with the acquisition of Pyrojunkies.

The company's long-term expertise and experienced staff mean it can specialise in complex, large-scale solutions. ER Productions has worked regularly at large-scale events for acts including Katy Perry, Calvin Harris, Metallica, Queen and Adam Lambert, London 2012 Olympics, America's Got Talent and Soundstorm Festival. ER Productions also delivers high-concept creative installations for corporate brands, including Samsung, Honda, Jaguar Land Rover and General Electric.

International Reach, In-House Expertise

The company has offices in the UK, EU, USA, Saudi Arabia and Australia allowing it to provide its services to an international client base.

In-house design capabilities mean the company is at the forefront of both product and production design and development. The company has led the development of laser technology for entertainment applications, with input from Technicians in the field, creating smaller, safer and more creatively flexible laser systems, and is recognised as an expert in this area. ER Productions owns the IP to leading laser technology.

ER Productions has won over 35 awards for outstanding laser displays. The company has also been awarded favourite SFX company at the TPI Awards twice, the only multi-discipline company to have been awarded the accolade, despite having only recently added the SFX service to the portfolio.

New & Custom
Products

ER Productions is primarily a service company which uses its own products and IP to provide a fully delivered Laser, Pyro and SFX show service. The company also provides its equipment as dry hire and sales. Products are manufactured for ER Productions by companies in the UK, Germany and Hungary.

We are constantly developing new products and effects for clients, including custom work.

Design &
Visualisation

ER Productions offer full design and visualisation services using WYSIWYG, Depence² and Finale 3D software packages.

When clients have a production in development we can produce render videos showing our effects in the true, scaled environment.

We can also offer rental of our bespoke visualisation suites in our new HQ. Both have been fitted with two Visualization Suites complete with Depence², WYSWYG and 4m wide 4k screens.
